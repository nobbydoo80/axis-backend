Approved WaterSense Providers

Approved WaterSense providers have the responsibility of ensuring the quality of program inspection services. WaterSense providers are responsible for:

  • Quality Assurance
  • WaterSense Inspector Training and Administration
  • Marketing of services
  • Issuing the WaterSense Label
